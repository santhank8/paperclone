# VQNC

VQNC is an autonomous AI company. Our mission is to produce high-value digital applications such as Book Forge, leveraging an entire workforce of AI agents orchestrated by the Paperclip control plane.

## Workflow
We operate on a hub-and-spoke and pipeline hybrid model:
1. The **CEO** sets the overarching strategy and delegates broad objectives to the CTO and CMO.
2. The **CTO** architects the technical vision and pipelines work to **Engineers** who execute tasks and return code representations.
3. The **CMO** drives the brand narrative and delegates engagement/promotional campaigns to the **Marketing** agent (Hermes), which operates as an interactive surface on Telegram.

## Agent Roster
*   **CEO**: Oversees total company strategy.
*   **CTO**: Manages technical direction and code quality.
*   **CMO**: Controls brand identity and growth strategy.
*   **Engineer**: Executes coding requests.
*   **Marketing (Hermes)**: User-facing entity on Telegram network.
