# Customer Success Weekly Review Template

**Owner:** Echo (Customer Success Lead)
**Purpose:** Standardized weekly metrics review and improvement tracking
**Frequency:** Every Friday or Monday (consistency matters)
**Duration:** 60 minutes per week

---

## Quick Overview

This template helps you review what happened last week, identify what needs to improve, and document actions for next week.

**Fill this out every Friday or Monday.** Make it a ritual. Consistency beats perfection.

## How to Use This Template

### Every Friday (30-60 minutes)

1. **Gather data** (10 min)
   - Open support@diybrand.app and count emails
   - Check feedback database for ratings
   - Pull analytics for FAQ/guide views

2. **Fill in Part 1-3** (20 min)
   - Email volume
   - Feedback ratings
   - Current metrics

3. **Fill in Part 4-5** (15 min)
   - FAQ gaps from this week
   - Success stories
   - Next week's actions

4. **Share summary** (5 min)
   - Post weekly summary to Slack/Discord
   - Flag any critical issues

5. **Save for records** (store in shared folder)

### Every 4 Weeks (30 minutes)

1. Look at all 4 weekly reviews
2. Fill in "Monthly Review" section
3. Identify trends
4. Report to leadership

## Common Patterns to Watch

### Red Flags (Escalate Immediately)

```
☐ Same question asked 3+ times → FAQ is failing
☐ 1-star feedback jumping (e.g., 1 → 10 in a week) → Something broke
☐ Support volume spiking (e.g., 2/week → 10/week) → Major issue
☐ Email response time >24 hours → Staffing issue
☐ Multiple refund requests for same reason → Product issue
```

### Green Flags (Continue What You're Doing)

```
☐ Support volume <5/week → Self-serve is working
☐ Feedback avg 4.5+/5 → Customers happy
☐ FAQ searches >25/week → Customers finding answers
☐ 0 repeat questions → Documentation is working
☐ 80%+ response time <4 hours → Team is responsive
```

## Success Looks Like

After 4 weeks of weekly reviews:

✅ Support email volume is stable or declining
✅ You can see which FAQ items are working (no repeat questions)
✅ You know which guides need clarification (customers still emailing)
✅ Product team has a clear list of bugs to fix
✅ You've found and fixed at least 5 FAQ gaps

After 8 weeks:

✅ Support volume trending toward <5/week
✅ Feedback ratings consistent 4.5+/5
✅ FAQ is mostly complete (rare new questions)
✅ New customers rarely email support
✅ Every question is either in FAQ or being added
