## General Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Personalize with customer names
- Keep tone friendly and conversational
- Link to help resources (FAQ, guides)
- Offer next steps
- Follow up if you promise something

❌ **Don't:**
- Use robotic language ("Dear valued customer")
- Make customers feel dumb for asking
- Blame them for issues
- Leave them hanging — always give next steps

---

## Tracking

When you use a template:
1. Add customer email to support list
2. Note issue category (getting started, technical, refund, etc.)
3. If new pattern emerges, update FAQ/guides to prevent future tickets
4. Goal: **Zero repeat support tickets** — if someone asks, everyone needs it in the docs
