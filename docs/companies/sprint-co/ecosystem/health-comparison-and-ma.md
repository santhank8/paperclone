# Health Comparison and Merger Protocol

Purpose: operationalize roadmap tasks 153 and 156.

## 1. Company Health Comparison (Task 153)

Benchmark dimensions:
- Delivery velocity
- Quality and defect escape rate
- Budget efficiency
- Governance stability (escalations, dissent outcomes)
- Resilience performance (incident recovery time)

Comparison policy:
- Compare only companies with similar mission profile and phase maturity
- Use rolling 4-sprint windows, not single sprint snapshots

Output:
- Quarterly ecosystem benchmark report with percentile ranks

## 2. Merger and Acquisition Protocol (Task 156)

Pre-merge checklist:
- Mission compatibility assessment
- Agent overlap and role conflict map
- Knowledge asset inventory
- Budget and governance policy reconciliation

Execution phases:
- Phase A: Treaty and governance alignment
- Phase B: Agent roster merge and role remap
- Phase C: Artifact and precedent consolidation
- Phase D: Stabilization sprint with heightened monitoring

Success criteria:
- No critical incidents during first merged sprint
- Handoff continuity maintained above threshold
- Unified governance constitution ratified

Output artifacts:
- Merger plan document
- Post-merge retrospective after first 2 sprints
