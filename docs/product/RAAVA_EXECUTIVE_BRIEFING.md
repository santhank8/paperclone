## Page 2: The Product

### What Is Raava?

Raava is an AI workforce platform that lets businesses hire, manage, and scale AI team members. Instead of configuring software or building automations, you browse pre-configured roles (Sales Assistant, Operations Manager, Customer Support, Data Analyst, Marketing Coordinator, or General Assistant), pick the one you need, name them, connect your tools, and they start working. The entire process takes under two minutes.

Each AI team member comes with a defined personality, a set of integrated tools, and the ability to work autonomously on tasks you assign. They follow up on leads, draft responses, pull reports, manage schedules, and escalate to you when human judgment is needed. You manage your AI team from a dashboard that looks and feels like a team directory -- not a developer console.

### How It Works: The 4-Step Hire Flow

| Step | What Happens | Time |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Create Your Company** | Enter your company name and your role. One-time setup. | 15 seconds |
| **2. Choose a Role** | Browse six pre-configured roles. Each card shows what the team member does and which tools they use. Pick the one you need. | 20 seconds |
| **3. Connect Your Tools** | Paste API keys for the services your team member will use (Gmail, HubSpot, Zendesk, etc.). Credentials are stored in a secure vault. You can skip this step and add them later. | 30 seconds |
| **4. Name and Launch** | Give your team member a name, review their first task, and hit "Hire." They start working immediately. | 15 seconds |

**Total time from zero to a working AI team member: under 2 minutes.**

### The Vision: 4-Tier Architecture

This is where Raava's long-term value becomes clear. The platform is designed to scale from a single AI team member to a full AI-powered organization.

| Tier | What It Is | Example | Status |
|---|---|---|---|

| **Pod** | A small team of 2-4 AI members coordinating on related work | Sales Assistant + Customer Support + Data Analyst working the full customer lifecycle | Launching Q3 2026 |

Each tier builds on the last. Solo proves the value of a single hire. Pod proves that AI team members can collaborate. Swarm proves that AI can run departments. Full Org is the endgame: a business where every repetitive function is handled by AI, and humans focus on strategy, relationships, and judgment calls.

## Page 6: Technology

### Architecture Overview

Raava runs on a managed AI infrastructure stack purpose-built for multi-tenant AI workforce operations. Each AI team member gets an isolated compute environment, a secure credential vault, and a runtime that can read email, update CRMs, query databases, and execute multi-step workflows -- all without the customer touching any infrastructure.

### Platform Components

| Layer | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Management Layer** | The brain of the platform. Handles team member provisioning, task assignment, scheduling, billing, and the dashboard experience. Exposes 95+ API endpoints. Orchestrates everything the user sees and does. |
| **Agent Runtime** | The execution engine. Each AI team member runs inside a dedicated, isolated environment with pre-loaded skills (email, CRM, document drafting, etc.) and a defined personality that guides how they work. Tasks are assigned, executed, and reported back through a structured protocol. |
| **Managed Infrastructure** | The compute layer. Isolated virtual machines per customer tenant. Provisioned on demand, scaled automatically, monitored continuously. No shared state between tenants. |
| **Security Layer** | All credentials stored in a 1Password vault -- never in plaintext, never in a database. Injected at runtime via secure environment variables. Full audit trail of every action every team member takes. Tenant isolation at the infrastructure level. |

### Key Technical Facts

- **95+ API endpoints** powering the full platform
- **Tenant isolation:** Each customer's AI team members run in dedicated compute environments
- **Credential security:** 1Password vault integration -- API keys are never stored in plaintext
- **Audit trail:** Every action, every task, every decision logged and reviewable
- **Scalability:** Architecture supports 1 to 20+ AI team members per customer
- **Full authentication and authorization** system with role-based access control
