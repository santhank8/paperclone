Telegram Integration

Full two-way control from your phone.

The IronWorks Telegram bot gives you a direct line to your entire AI workforce. Ask any agent for a status update, assign new work, approve blocked items, or trigger a playbook run. Agents also push notifications to you proactively — blocked items, completed goals, and urgent alerts come through without you having to ask.

  • Natural language commands — no slash command syntax required
  • Proactive alerts for blockers, goal overdue, and agent errors
  • Assign work, close issues, and approve decisions without opening a browser
  • Available on Starter, Growth, and Business plans
What you can do from Telegram
"Status on project Alpha"
Gets current issue count, blocked items, and last agent activity
"Run the Security Audit playbook"

Email Integration

Configurable alerts. No inbox noise.

Email integration covers two directions: IronWorks sends you structured alerts when things need your attention, and agents can send emails on your behalf as part of playbook workflows. You control exactly which alerts you receive and when — daily briefing, blocker alerts, overdue goals, and playbook completions are all individually configurable.

  • Daily operational briefing with everything you need to start the day
  • Agents can send emails to clients and stakeholders via playbooks
  • Alert thresholds you control — not every minor event triggers email
  • Available on all plans including Starter
