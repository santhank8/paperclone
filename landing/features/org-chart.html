Delegation Chains

Work flows down the chain. Reports flow back up.

When you assign a goal to your CEO agent, it breaks the work into sub-tasks and delegates to the appropriate direct reports. The CTO gets engineering tasks. The CMO gets marketing tasks. Each agent delegates further to their own reports. You only intervene when blockers require human decisions.

  • Managers break down goals into issues for their direct reports
  • Completed work rolls up as status updates to the manager level
  • Approval gates for sensitive decisions based on reporting level
  • Escalation rules: unresolved blockers surface to manager automatically

Customization

Every agent is configurable. Nothing is locked in.

Start with a team pack and adjust as you need. Rename agents, change their LLM provider, adjust their approval authority, update their system prompt, and move them up or down in the hierarchy. Each agent configuration persists until you change it.

  • Assign different LLM models to different agents to optimize cost
  • Set per-agent spending limits and approval authority thresholds
  • Custom system prompts with access to Knowledge Base context
  • Add and remove agents from the org chart at any time
