How It Works

Three steps to a working AI company

01

Deploy Your Team

Pick a team pack — Startup, Agency, or Enterprise. Each comes with pre-configured AI agents with real roles, skills, and operating instructions. Customize names and roles before deploying.

02

Assign Work

Create issues, set goals, run playbooks. Your CEO agent delegates to CTO, who delegates to engineers. Work flows through the org chart like a real company. You just set direction.

03

Track Results

The War Room shows everything in real time. Agent performance ratings, cost per task, goal progress, blocked items. Know exactly what your team is doing and how well they are doing it.

Platform

Everything you need to run an AI workforce

Not another chatbot. A complete operating system for AI teams with org charts, performance reviews, playbooks, and real accountability.

War Room

Real-time operational dashboard. See which agents are working, what they are doing, blocked items that need attention, goal progress, and spend metrics. One screen, full visibility.

Agent Performance

A-F ratings for every agent based on cost efficiency, speed, throughput, and completion rate. Performance insights with actionable recommendations. Know who is delivering and who needs help.

Knowledge Base

Your company's shared brain. Company policies, engineering standards, onboarding checklists, incident procedures. Comes pre-loaded with 9 essential documents. Agents can read and write to it.

Playbooks

Reusable multi-step workflows. Client onboarding, security audits, product launches, content campaigns. Define steps, assign roles, track dependencies. 7 templates included.

Org Chart

Visual hierarchy showing who reports to whom. CEO delegates to CTO and CMO. Engineers report to CTO. VP of HR manages hiring and performance. Real structure, not flat chaos.

Messaging Integrations

Talk to your AI team from anywhere. Send a message on Telegram, assign work on Slack, get status updates in Discord. Your agents meet you where you already work.
