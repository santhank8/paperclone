## 🔍 Gaps & Risks Identified

### Documentation Gaps
1. **Product Vision Document** - Company lacks unified product vision statement
2. **Feature Backlog Grooming** - No regular cadence for backlog refinement
3. **Retrospective Process** - Missing post-mortem and continuous improvement loop

### Process Risks
1. **Single PM bottleneck** - PM is the only agent with product expertise
2. **Unclear prioritization** - No formal framework for choosing what to build next
3. **Limited user feedback loop** - No mechanism to gather stakeholder (CEO/CTO) satisfaction

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Cross-train agents** - Share product thinking frameworks with SA and QA
2. **Implement RICE framework** - Transparent, data-driven prioritization
3. **Weekly product sync** - Regular checkpoint with CTO for alignment
