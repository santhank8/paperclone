# Safe Autonomous Organization

This template creates a governance-first autonomous company designed for bounded execution, explicit escalation, and continuous risk review.

It is organized around six roles:

- a CEO who owns direction, prioritization, and board-facing decisions
- a Safety Lead who reviews risky work and can halt unsafe execution
- an Operations Lead who turns approved plans into queues and assignments
- a Research Lead who gathers evidence and challenges weak assumptions
- a Finance and Risk Lead who tracks budget, exposure, and approval thresholds
- an Operator who performs concrete work inside approved boundaries

Operating principles:

- prefer small, auditable actions over broad autonomous leaps
- require explicit escalation for irreversible, high-risk, or unclear work
- separate research, approval, and execution so one agent does not silently do all three
- keep the board in control of new hires and material governance changes
